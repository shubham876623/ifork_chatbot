## About iFork Mackay

iFork Mackay is a local delivery and transport service based in Mackay, Queensland. We specialise in transporting heavy, bulky, or palletised goods using a truck fitted with a rear-mounted Moffett all-terrain forklift. This allows us to safely load and unload materials directly at site, even in locations where standard freight trucks cannot operate.

Our services are commonly used by builders, construction companies, landscapers, pool builders, hardware suppliers, flooring companies, and other businesses that need reliable site deliveries.

Because our truck carries its own forklift, we can deliver goods directly to site without requiring additional unloading equipment. This is particularly useful for construction sites, residential developments, rural properties, and locations without forklifts.

iFork Mackay operates under MLMA Holdings Pty Ltd (ABN 38 687 306 850).

Our goal is to make transporting goods simple, efficient, and safe for local businesses.

## Truck and Moffett Capabilities

iFork Mackay operates a truck fitted with a truck mounted Moffett all-terrain forklift, which allows us to deliver and unload materials directly at site without requiring additional equipment.

The key advantage of a Moffett forklift is that it travels with the truck and can be quickly unloaded to safely move materials from the truck to the required location on site.

**Typical Capabilities**

Our setup is designed for transporting and unloading palletised or bulky goods commonly used in the construction and building industries. The Moffett forklift allows us to:

- Unload materials directly from the truck
- Deliver goods onto construction sites
- Place pallets close to where they are needed
- Access sites where standard forklifts are not available
- Operate on uneven or rough ground conditions

This makes our service ideal for builders, suppliers, and trades who require materials delivered directly to site.

**Payload Capacity**

Our truck can carry up to approximately 12 tonnes of freight, depending on the type of goods being transported. This capacity allows us to transport large loads of building materials such as: palletised tiles, bricks and blocks, timber, steel, landscaping materials, construction supplies.

**Forklift Capacity**

The Moffett forklift typically lifts up to around 2 to 2.5 tonnes, depending on load type and lift position. Actual lifting capacity may vary based on: load size and stability, ground conditions, reach distance, height of placement. Our driver will always assess the safest way to unload goods at each site.

**Site Access**

For safe operation, the truck requires reasonable site access and space to deploy the forklift. Customers should ensure: adequate space for the truck to park, stable ground conditions for forklift operation, no overhead obstructions, clear access to the unloading area.

If you are unsure whether your site is suitable, we recommend discussing the job with us prior to booking so we can confirm whether our equipment will work for your delivery.

## Pricing

**Standard rate:** $150 per hour + GST.

Time is charged from when the vehicle departs our depot until it returns to the depot.

If the job begins at a location other than our depot, travel time from the depot to the pickup location will be calculated using Google Maps under normal traffic conditions. The same applies for the return trip.

**Chargeable time includes:** Travel time, loading time, transport time, unloading time, any required waiting time.

**Minimum Charge**

Our minimum call-out charge is 1.5 hours. This means the minimum invoice for any job will be: 1.5 hours × $150 + GST.

**Quotes and Estimates**

Yes, we can provide quotes or estimates. Many jobs require a rough estimate prior to booking. If you would like a quote, simply provide the following details:

- Pickup location
- Delivery location
- Description of goods
- Estimated weight or size
- Whether goods are palletised
- Whether assistance is required for loading or unloading
- Site access details

We will review the information and provide a realistic estimate based on travel time and expected unloading conditions. Because site conditions can vary, some jobs are still charged on an hourly basis even if an estimate is provided.

## Safety and SWMS

Safety is extremely important. iFork Mackay operates with established safety procedures and can provide Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS) if required.

Customers are responsible for ensuring: safe access for the truck and forklift, adequate ground conditions for forklift operation, no overhead hazards or obstructions, clear unloading areas.

If our driver determines that conditions are unsafe for unloading, alternative arrangements will need to be made.

**Site Requirements:** Adequate space for the truck to park, stable ground for forklift operation, clear access paths, no low overhead obstacles, no underground hazards. If unloading is unsafe due to site conditions, our driver may decline the unload for safety reasons. In this case, time on site is still chargeable.

## Preparing Goods for Transport

Goods should be: properly palletised, securely strapped, stable and safe to lift with a forklift. We may refuse to transport goods that are unsafe or inadequately prepared. If a pickup cannot proceed due to unsafe packaging or preparation, the minimum call-out charge still applies.
